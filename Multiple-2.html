1 The situation

Little Socium wanted to revamp the customer experience at a park where children get to experience adult jobs and learn about how society works. As a new market entrant in children's theme parks, the client faced significant challenges in leveraging China’s latest development trends in mobile internet and consumption technology.

2 What we did

To deliver a complete O2O experience and increase visitor’s interest and participation PwC designed and implemented a unique customer experience with a diverse range of technologies (e.g. iBeacons, smart bands) to streamline regular activities, such as ticket reservation, shopping, sharing data on social media, and also address and derive innovation with existing pain points such as the ability to track the location of their child, providing routing guidance to a desired location from their svmart phone.
